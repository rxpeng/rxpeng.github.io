Biography

My research interests lie at the intersection of digital advertising and health decision-making. The primary focus of my research is on users’ experience, engagement, and evaluations of social media ads. To date, my work has also involved studying designs and effects of health promotion campaigns on social media. Some of my research involved utilizing computational tools and techniques to investigate how users process and interact with digital health information. To better prepare for the future research characterized by the apex of meta-interactivity, I envision my research expanding into the applications of Extended Reality in strategic communication.

I embrace diverse methodologies to better match the evolving media environment, including experimental design, survey research, sensors/tracking technologies and computational methods.
